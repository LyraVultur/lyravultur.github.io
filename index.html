ABOUT

Lyra has been a fan of making games ever since she was 12, and this has led to many small video game projects over the years. She also adores chao (from the Sonic the Hedgehog games) and cats (from real life). Both these things often show up in her work. With the help of some friends, she is currently working on a much larger project than ever before.
